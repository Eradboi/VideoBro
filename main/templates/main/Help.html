VideoBro Help Documentation

Youtube Video Download

1. Paste the Youtube video or shorts url in the search bar above, on the top of the webpage
2. Wait for the webpage to reload and show your desired video
3. Choose to download as either video or audio
4. Repaste the url on the following page and click download
5. Wait for the video or audio to download on your device

Instagram Video Download

1. Paste your desired Instagram Url and wait for the download to be completed
2. NOTE You cannot access contents from private accounts

Youtube Playlist Download

1. When downloading or checking Youtube playlists on VideoBro for mobile devices especially, make sure the link to the playlist starts with a https://www.youtube.com/watch?v= and not https://youtube.com/playlist?list=.
2. While downloading playlists on VideoBro, you need a strong internet connection.
3. Some playlists may not download due to age restrictions on them, making it necessary to login before accessing them.
4. If one or more videos on a playlist fails to download, then the whole playlist will not be downloaded on your device.
